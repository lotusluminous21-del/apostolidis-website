# Email Delivery Setup Guide (Resend Domain Verification)

To allow the Apostolidis website to send emails on behalf of `@apostolidisconstructions.gr` (even if no physical inbox exists for it), we must verify domain ownership with our email provider, **Resend**.

This process involves adding a few DNS records to the domain's configuration. This securely authenticates your domain and ensures emails aren't flagged as spam.

---

## Part 1: Generating the DNS Records (For Lotus)
1. Go to [resend.com](https://resend.com) and log into your account.
2. On the left sidebar, click **Domains**.
3. Click the **Add Domain** button in the top right.
4. Enter `apostolidisconstructions.gr` and select a region (e.g., EU).
5. Click **Add**.
6. Resend will now display a table of exactly 1 MX record and 2 TXT records (DKIM and SPF) you need to add.
7. Note these records or take a screenshot and send them to your Domain Manager.

---

## Part 2: Configuring the DNS (For the Domain Manager)
The website owner will provide you with a set of DNS records (DKIM, SPF, and potentially a bounce MX record) generated by Resend.

Please log into the DNS management console for `apostolidisconstructions.gr` (e.g., GoDaddy, Namecheap, Papaki, Cloudflare) and add the records exactly as provided. 

### Expected Records Format:

**1. DKIM Record**
This securely signs the emails sent from the website.
* **Type:** `TXT` (or `CNAME` depending on the exact Resend configuration)
* **Name / Host:** `resend._domainkey` (or similar)
* **Value:** *(A long string of characters provided by Resend)*

**2. SPF Record**
This authorizes Resend's servers to send emails on your behalf.
* **Type:** `TXT`
* **Name / Host:** `bounce` (or `@`)
* **Value:** `v=spf1 include:amazonses.com ~all` (or similar value provided by Resend)

**3. MX Record (For Bounces)**
This handles email bounce tracking so we know if an email failed to deliver.
* **Type:** `MX`
* **Name / Host:** `bounce`
* **Value:** `feedback-smtp.eu-west-1.amazonses.com` (or similar value provided by Resend)
* **Priority:** `10`

> **Note on Propagation:** After adding these records in the DNS manager, it may take 5 to 60 minutes for the changes to propagate across the internet. 

---

### Verification
Once the Domain Manager has confirmed the DNS changes:
1. Go back to your [Resend Domains Dashboard](https://resend.com/domains).
2. Click on the `apostolidisconstructions.gr` domain.
3. Click the **Verify DNS Records** button.
4. When the status turns green (**Verified**), the contact form on your website will automatically start delivering emails from `info@apostolidisconstructions.gr` without any further code changes!
